plant-lover, creative, and vinyl collector

I’m a life-long learner; whether that's expanding my coding skills, reading or taking up a new creative endeavor (currently pottery making!). When I’m not coding you'll find me adding to my plant and vinyl collection with iced coffee or bubble tea in hand.
